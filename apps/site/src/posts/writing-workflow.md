---
title: A Lightweight Writing Workflow
date: 2025-08-16
tags: [process, writing]
---

My workflow favors tiny daily moves:

- Open a fresh note with a UTC date.
- Write one insight or one paragraph.
- If momentum appears, keep going; if not, ship the small win.

Tooling keeps out of the way and rewards consistency (streaks, simple stats, calendar heatmap).
