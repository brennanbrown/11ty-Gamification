A quick, practical guide to writing posts in Markdown. This page demonstrates the most common syntax you’ll need when writing in your IndieGarden.

## Headings

Use `#` through `######` for headings:

# H1
## H2
### H3

## Emphasis

- Italic: *text* or _text_
- Bold: **text** or __text__
- Strike: ~~text~~
